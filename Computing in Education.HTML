Education

Computing has revolutionised the way in which people are educated. The very concept of being able learn in an interactive environment form the comfort of your own home is something that can only be accomplished thanks to the many afforadances of modern technology. Aside from pure convenience, there are many other benefits that come from this as well.

Examples:

  • Zoom
  • Online Courses
  • Free textbooks
  • Organisation

Online Resources:

Given the freedom and flexibility of the internet, the advantages of online resources are near endless. Within the context of education, this may allow for students and learners to research any information at ease through the use of digital textbooks, articles, video sharing platforms such as facebook and many other tools. Not only is much of this information free to access, but also quick and easy to find. Another example of useful digital resources for education are video-calling applications such as Zoom and Microsoft Teams. These allow for possibilities such as remote lessons or lectures.

Arithmetic:

Within education, the way that subjects such as mathematics have been taught has been revolutionised thanks to developments in technology. From the use of calculators to instantly work out equations, to applications that can visually graph out data, student are able to learn in an evironment where they are not hindered by spending time on simple but laborous calculations.
